Premananda Centre provides a real and loving home for needy children, especially orphans, to assist
them with their right to live and grow up to be healthy, well-educated, and independent individuals,
free from possible future exploition.

Premananda assures the following :
1.To provide children with healthy and hygienic housing.
2.To provide children with clean and neat clothing.
3.To provide children the scope for physical development through nutritious food, sports and games.
4.To provide children quality education.
5.To provide children spiritual development through regular prayer and frequent mass.

Currently, Premananda consists of 3 Centres :

A main Ongole city campus for girls.
A rural Kothapatnam farm Centre for boys.
A third Centre is in the Ramnagar 10th line area of Ongole.
