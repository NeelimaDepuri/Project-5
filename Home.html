Premananda serves 120 children and young adults each year in below three centres.

1.The main Ongole Pothurajupalem Centre (a short walk from the Rail Station) accommodates girls.



2.Boys are housed at the Kothapatnam 10-acre farm Centre near the Bay of Bengal,
15 kilometers from Ongole. Most of the higher education students reside in their respective college hostels.



3.The Ongole Ramnagar (10th Line) Centre is a ten-minute drive from the main Ongole campus.

OUR MISSION

Premananda, which means Home of Love and Joy, provides a real and loving home for needy children, especially orphans,
to assist them with their right to live and grow up to be healthy, well-educated, and independent individuals free from possible future exploitation.
