Founder Father Jojaiah Chopparapu (1960-2008) was a dedicated Nellore Diocesan Catholic priest who set up Premananda Centre,
Co-founder Hawaii Chaminade University Professor Jon James’s involvement in India
The First Premananda Centre was dedicated on 28 June 1998 with James and Italian Vilma Punti from Rome in attendance (Vilma personally,
along with many of her friends, raised a considerable sum which led to the Ongole Centre’s completion).


Premananda then expanded–with the generous assistance of mostly Catholic donors from UK and Europe–to add a
10-acre Kothapatnam farm Centre in 2005 and a third Centre in the Ramnagar section of Ongole.
In 2007 Chris and Jane Hewson gave a huge sum to help build this latter Centre and they, along with Jon James,
were on hand for its dedication. Today the non-college girls reside at the original main Ongole Centre campus while the Kothapatnam farm Centre houses all the non-college boys.
All the higher education young adults live at their respective college hostels.
